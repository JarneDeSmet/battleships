How to play

Player 1 drags his boats on the grid, when all boats are in place you click confirm. Player 2 then does the exact same

When both fields are set, player 1 can start by clicking a grid place. If you hit your opponent's boat you will see the grid place (your target) go red and an explosion sound will play. When you miss, it will turn blue and a splash sound will play.

After shooting a pop-up screen will appear, that is when you give the device to your opponent. they then click ready to be able to shoot.

Each boat takes up multiple grid places and therefore has to be hit multiple times. When you have destroyed all your opponent's boats (17 hits total) you win.
